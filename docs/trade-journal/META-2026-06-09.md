### Lessons Learned (from this review)

1. **Stop tightness:** A 0.91-ATR stop on a $19 ATR high-beta stock is too tight. The stop was set at max(2×ATR, swing_low, 20d_low, -5%). With ATR=$18.84, 2×ATR=$37.68 below entry = $555.52, which is BELOW the current price. The max() formula picked the swing_low or 20d_low rather than 2×ATR — this needs investigation.

2. **Thesis archiving is mandatory, not optional.** 5 of 8 fields were unrecorded. Every Hermes proposal must be persisted to a durable artifact before approval.

3. **Stop-breach response latency:** Breach occurred Jun 10, review initiated Jun 11. The 30-min RTH target is achievable with automated breach detection but requires a standing policy.
